Before Your Arrival

You can sign in for a spanish course given at ITBA, it starts two weeks before the beggining of the semester. But you should know it has an additional cost. You can continue with your course during the semester

You can choose courses from all the programs. Once the semester starts, you will have two weeks to decide whether you like the course or not, and make changes to your course program.

Once you're Here

ITBA has three buildings: Sede Financiero, Sede Rectorado and Sede Distrito Tecnológico. The first one is located in the neighborhood of Microcentro, while the other ones are in Parque Patricios. You can have classes in any of them, depending on the courses you choose

Besides, ITBA has many student groups with different themes. They often have events and activities you can join.

- There are microwaves in the university, in case you need to bring your lunch. But if you don’t bring your own, you can also buy food inside the buldings, or at any restaurant close to them and bring it back to the university.

- You can join the CEITBA organization, which has different discounts on gyms, and offers some sport classes, such as football, swimming lessons and basketball.
