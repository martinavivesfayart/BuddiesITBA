About Us

Buddies is all about making friends, exploring Buenos Aires, and having a great time together! Whether you're from near or far, we’re here to help you feel at home and create memories you’ll never forget.

  1. Home
  2. About Us

Buddies is a student organization from ITBA aimed at helping incoming students adapt to both the university and Buenos Aires. Each exchange student is paired with an Argentinian student from ITBA, their Buddy,to provide guidance and support during their stay.

  • Create lifelong friendships between local and international students.
  • Offer cultural events, city tours, and networking opportunities.
  • Help students adapt to life in Buenos Aires through personalized support.

The program provides both Argentinian and exchange students the opportunity to meet people from all over the world, fostering an environment of cultural exchange and mutual growth.
